GoodRx prices

GoodRx is NOT insurance. It is a discount program used in place of insurance. It is most useful for generic medications.

  1. GoodRx price is pharmacy dependent.
  2. Go to GoodRx.com and type your medication.
  3. Review costs at different pharmacies.
  4. Select how to receive the coupon.
  5. Present coupon at pharmacy.
Cost Plus Drugs prices

Cost Plus Drugs offers significantly reduced pricing on generics shipped to your home.

  1. Create a free account.
  2. Review medication prices.
  3. Have provider send prescription.
  4. Add prescriptions to cart.
  5. Checkout (small shipping fee applies).
