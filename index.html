Eliminating barriers and providing solutions to patients obtaining medications

  1. INSTITUTIONS is for your healthcare proivder
  2. MEDICARE provides side by side comparisons of Medicare Part D and Advantage plans
  3. MEDICAID will take you to your state's Medicaid formulary drug list for what is covered
  4. VA will allow you to review the Veteran's Affairs formulary drug list for what is covered
  5. Unite Us has local Florida resources for things like food, shelters, clothing, psychiatric care, etc.
  6. GoodRx and Cost Plus Drugs are cost savings resources for generic medications to be used in place of your insurance

M.A.G.I.C. is intended for use by persons who have trouble affording ANY of their brand name medications. MAGIC includes programs offered for > 650 medications through the drug manufacturers where your medication is sent to your home or your provider's office for FREE for an entire year. Each program that a person may be eligible for requires the following information for eligibility:

  1. Annual Income
  2. Number of persons in household
  3. Insurance status
Prescription insurance coverage accepted:
  1. Medicare (A, B, D and Advantage plans)
  2. Uninsured
